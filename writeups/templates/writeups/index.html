Welcome

Welcome to Moonlight. A community of young African Litrary enthusiast. We are dedicated to supporting young aspiring authors across africa by providing a platform where every member can express themeselves through their arts and get feedback from people who share similar goal and aspiration. By so doing, enjoying themselves and honing their arts at the same time.

Here is a poem to welcome you
The Downing Kite
SolidStreams

We snak our young chests
Into the forest sharps
Seeking for the finest of sticks
To propel our kite

We crafted our kite
Daring as witched ravens
Camping in the clouds
And hope it was christenened

Then we found the loudest of stars
Packed them in palm packs
And tied them to hopes back
As a parcel for God

Accept our hopes dearest Lord
Let us grow green branching skywards
To heights where our Hopes cannot touched
But Hope was punctured by hooded infidels

And we saw it downing
Descending heavily
Upon our worn cheeks
We the black children

And we retired home wearing
Our cheecks between our palms
